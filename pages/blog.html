Technology
Introduction
“It’s not that we use technology, we live technology”. This quote by Geoffrey Reggio portrays how technology has come to become an indispensable part of our life. Development has been a major pursuit of humanity for a long time now. People havetried to improve their ways of life by innovating numerous and diverse things. One of the greatest advancements of the times has been technology. We now like in a world where every teen has a mobile device in their hand, where paper books are being replaced by e-books and surprisingly where even toddler are lured by gadgets! Computer technology in particular has taken the world by storm. Whether its forecasting the weather or performing even a minor calculation, there is no denying in the fact that technology has become our true savior.
In the present context, the positive impact technology has put forth is boundless. Here are some of the benefits in miscellaneous fields that computer technology has to offer:

Acquiring Information
Via the Internet, online reference material, such as Encyclopedia, Wikipedia offer vast amounts of content supplemented by engaging multimedia and interactive links. E-book collections offer thousands of texts, and the sheer number of online articles and journals devoted to every imaginable topic make research efficient and highly rewarding.

Communication
From post mail which takes upto months to E-mail which can be delivered instantaneously, communication has evolved as per the advancement of technology. Social networking sites like Facebook, Twitter, Skype allows one to voice their opinions and also communicate with individual in any corner of the world.

Teaching and Education
Through the application of computer technology, learning can take place online where students attain knowledge virtually. Such facility has helped students, despite their economic status, assuage their craving for wisdom. Furthermore, the use multimedia has made learning more interactive boosting the efficiency of students to understand effectively.
Advantages
Disadvantages
The immense advantages of technology has nullified the its downside in the contemporary society. Some drawbacks of technological advancement can be illustrated as:

Depedency
Having access to a treasure-trove of resources is wonderful, but it can also create a condition of dependency. As most technological discoveries aim to reduce human effort, the human is becoming obsolete as processes become automated and jobs are made redundant.

Social Disconnect
There is an increasing tendency for people to socialize and communicate via digital devices rather than through real life contact. This can easily lead to a sense of disconnect and isolation. Studies have suggested that the lack of real life contact is causing depression and other forms of mental illness in many people.

Cyber Vulnerability
The outgrowth of Internet has unknowingly paved its path toward dangers as people are being a victim of Cyber-crime on a daily basis. It has also made us question the security of confidential data and millions of dollars that are in risk due to unethical means through the use of computers.
We humans have thrived and left no stone unturned to quench our thirst for more and more advanced technology in hope to ease our burdensome lifestyle. It is crystal clear that the modern world does indeed place too much reliance on technology. On the one hand it does make our lives easier; but at what expense? Undoubtedly, the pros of technology outweighs its cons by a huge margin. But it does not mean that the negative aspects are something to be overseen. In my opinion, whilst we relish the boon of technology, we must be totally conscious to keep it within its limit so that it doesn’t turn into bane.
Verdict
